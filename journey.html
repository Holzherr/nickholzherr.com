Key moments

  • 1986Born in Switzerland
  • 2008Co-Go — coffee kiosks at Aston University, the first business
  • 2010Air HR founded — HR tech for small businesses
  • 2012The Apprentice final on BBC One — pitches Whisk to Lord Sugar; £170k first round closes weeks later
  • 2013–14Whisk raises seed and strategic VC; first office and a company football team
  • 2016The hard year — restructure, back to a 10-person team, 100% focus on revenue
  • 2017–19Profitable growth as the B2B food platform behind the world's biggest food brands
  • 2019Three inbound acquisition offers — Samsung wins; team grows from 30 to 100+ in nine months

  • 2022Upwork suspends operations in Russia — with roughly 60% of the team based there, a scramble to keep everyone; Air HR sold to a US group
  • 2023Whisk relaunches globally as Samsung Food
  • 2024CES Innovation Award; on the SDC keynote stage
  • 2025GitLaw founded — $3M pre-seed led by Jackson Square Ventures
1980sSwitzerland

Born and raised in Switzerland — mountains, skiing, and a first taste of doing things properly.

Photo to comeChildhood in Switzerland — skiing in the Alps.
2008First business

Co-Go — a coffee business and the first company, started while studying at Aston University in Birmingham.

2010Air HR

Air HR — HR software for small businesses, founded in Whisk's early days and run alongside it before its sale to a US group in 2022.

2012The Apprentice

Finalist on BBC's The Apprentice with Lord Sugar — pitching what would become Whisk in front of eight million people, and landing on magazine front covers.

2012–19Whisk

Building Whisk from Birmingham's iCentrum into an AI food platform used by the world's biggest food brands — one of the earliest fully distributed teams, growing to 120 people worldwide.

2019Acquisition

Samsung acquires Whisk. The team joins Samsung's ecosystem, and the platform starts its journey toward every Samsung kitchen.

2019–25Samsung Food

Leading the platform inside Samsung for six years — relaunched globally as Samsung Food in 2023, on stage at Samsung Developer Conference, CES Innovation Award winner.

2025–GitLaw

Venture three: GitLaw — the AI agent for contracts. Drafting, reviewing, and managing legal documents for growing businesses.
